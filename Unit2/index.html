Design Challenge

Which two websites are you most inspired by?

I was most inspired by yodabaz.com and operaballet.nl To me both sites were well designed and instantly caught my eye.

How do they exhibit effective use of space, size or balance?

The yodobaz portfolio site relies on a strong focal point to engage the user immediately. It achieves this by having the main clickable content clustered into one spot there by creating lots of negative space. The designer also used the size of the buttons to create a distinct hiearchy.

The opera site also uses focal point and hierarchy to lead the users eye to what is important. I am able to realize the huge text is probably the name of the opera,the smaller links will lead me to more info about the opera. Furthermore the "i" icon placed at the bottom encourages the user to scroll down creating a flow.

What makes them similar and different?

Both home pages use simple design to make it visually easy to navigate but also use video to bring life and complexity to the overall look of the site. One difference between the two sites is the use of flow. The ballet website use a well placed "i" icon at the bottom to guide the users eye to the bottom making the user want to explore and scroll down.
